  2. Why does the time displayed on the past patterns and search patterns page different than the regular trading days?

    Because we use UTC time which is more convenient and universal for members all over the world. UTC time is 5 hours ahead of the eastern standard time. To understand more about UTC time, click here.

    Because we believe that every investor/trader has the right to access the information they need/want in order to become a successful trader. We try to bring our fee as low as possible, so that this right comes true to all investors/traders and potential ones when it comes to stock pattern search.

  5. Is it safe to use credit card on your site?

  6. How do I cancel my plan?

    All plans when first purchased are non-recurring unless you check the automatically renew box and submitted it. To cancel a plan, simply click the submit button below the plans table with the automatically renew box unchecked.
